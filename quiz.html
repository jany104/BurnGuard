🧠 Burnout Quiz

This quiz is adapted from the Maslach Burnout Inventory and WHO-5 Wellbeing Index to help you reflect on your mental energy levels.

  1. I feel emotionally drained after a day of work or study.
  2. I feel tired even after sleeping well.
  3. I find it hard to get excited about my daily tasks.
  4. I feel disconnected or unmotivated in the things I usually enjoy.
  5. I feel like I’m just going through the motions most days.
  6. I avoid tasks because they feel overwhelming.
  7. I doubt the value of what I’m doing.
  8. I feel I’m not achieving as much as I should.
  9. I have felt calm and relaxed in the last few days. (reverse scored)
  10. I have woken up feeling fresh and rested. (reverse scored)
